Our Process

Simple, documented, and verifiable.

Every transaction runs through written agreements and third-party title or closing companies.

  • You share basic property information (address, condition, timeline).
  • We review the property, local data, and recent comparable sales.
  • You receive a clear written offer with no obligation to accept.
  • If we agree, we sign a purchase agreement and open title.
  • A licensed title or closing company handles the transfer of funds and deed.
Verification

How we structure verifiable closings.

We encourage homeowners, buyers, and partners to verify who they’re working with. Here are straightforward ways to confirm transaction details.

  • Confirm that your purchase and sale agreement lists the correct entity name and contact information.
  • Call the title or closing company handling the transaction to confirm that Exceptional Investments is the buyer or seller on record.
  • Ask for our preferred closing attorney or title contact to verify details and wiring instructions before sending any funds.

Note: Always independently confirm wiring instructions and closing details directly with the title or closing company to protect yourself from fraud.
